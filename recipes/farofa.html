Toasted cassava flour

Description

Brazilian farofa is a quintessential Brazilian side dish! It pairs wonderfully with many dishes, like rice and beans, stews and barbecue! It's simple and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 thick strips of bacon (diced)
  • 2 tablespoons of cold butter
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 cup of white yuca/cassava flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
